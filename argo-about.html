Our history

Born from a shared vision.

Argo was founded on the belief that companies deserve analytics partners who are as invested in outcomes as they are.

The Beginning
Two engineers meet at Domo
Andrew Chaffin and John Stevens first crossed paths at Domo, a leading business intelligence platform. With combined experience across the full data stack, they recognized an enormous gap: companies were leaving analytics value on the table, every single day.
2020
Argo Analytics is founded
Fueled by a desire to help organizations unlock the full potential of their data, Andrew and John launched Argo — a cutting-edge analytics consulting firm focused on helping clients optimize their Domo experience and drive data-driven decision-making.
Growth
Partners become the engine
Rapid growth driven almost entirely by dedicated, satisfied customers. Our clients aren't just clients — they're longtime partners. This success enabled us to attract and hire the very best talent from our network, united by a shared commitment to delivering exceptional results.
Today
At the forefront of analytics consulting
A proven track record of enabling businesses to harness the power of data and achieve outstanding results. Our relentless pursuit of excellence has cemented our reputation as a trusted partner in data-driven success. And we're just getting started.
